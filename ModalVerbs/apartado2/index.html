LISTA DE VERBOS MODALES

Modals Uses Examples
can ability, request, permission, possibility She can speak Chinese
be able to ability, possibility Peter is able to ride a bike
should, ought to advice, opinion You should/ought to cut down on coffee
have to necessity, obligation He must relocate soon, his house has been demolished
mustn't prohibition People mustn't step on the grass
don't have to lack of obligation You don't have to come if you don't want
